Date format help

Formats use case-sensitive date-fns tokens:

M
Month without padding, such as 1
MM
Two-digit month, such as 01
d
Day without padding, such as 5
dd
Two-digit day, such as 05
yy
Two-digit year, resolved to the most recent non-future year
yyyy
Four-digit year, such as 2026

Examples: yyyy-MM-dd, dd.MM.yy, MM/dd/yy.
